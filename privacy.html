Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Privacy is the core of Tasknull, and that extends to this website. This policy explains what limited information is involved when you visit. We do not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, or wallet address to browse.

1. What we collect

  • Server & security logs. Our hosting provider may automatically process basic technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) to deliver the site, keep it secure, and prevent abuse.
  • Aggregate analytics (if enabled). We may use privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics to understand traffic in aggregate. This does not identify you personally.
  • No tracking cookies. The site does not set advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

2. What we do not collect

We do not collect personal profiles, and the protocol is designed so that completing or claiming a bounty does not link your identity to your payout. This website does not request or store your wallet address or private keys.

3. On-chain data is public

Any transaction you make on a public blockchain (such as Base) is recorded on that network, is outside our control, and is publicly visible by its nature. Tasknull’s design aims to keep your identity unlinkable, but you remain responsible for your own operational privacy.

4. Third-party services

  • Hosting. The site is served by Vercel, which processes request data as described above.
  • Fonts. Web fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address to deliver the font files.
  • External links. Pages we link to (e.g. explorers, social platforms) have their own privacy practices that we do not control.

5. Children

The Services are not directed to anyone under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above reflects the latest revision.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy can be raised through the official community links listed on the homepage.
