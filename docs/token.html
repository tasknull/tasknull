$TNULL token

$TNULL is the bounty reward layer, designed to live on Solana. Reward pools are funded in $TNULL and pay out to the proof, never to a doxxable signer — the privacy model holds by construction.

How rewards flow

A bounty poster locks a reward in $TNULL. A hunter completes the work and submits a proof. Once the proof verifies and the nullifier is fresh, the reward releases to the fresh payout address in the proof — with no on-chain link back to the hunter's identity key.

On-chain settlement is not live yet. Today the CLI demonstrates the full cryptographic lifecycle locally; the Solana program that performs real settlement is on the roadmap.
